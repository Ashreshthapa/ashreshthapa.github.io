My Favourite HTML Element

Body Tag

The Body Element is the most important element in HTML.

The contents of Body Elements are what actually get displayed to the visitor of the website or the document.

The body tag comes after Head tag.

Any styling you need to do on the body element ought to be placed into your CSS.

Furthermore, in fact, body is an incredible spot to put all the CSS that should affect the web page layouts as a whole: things like fonts and typography, text, background color, background image and many other things.
